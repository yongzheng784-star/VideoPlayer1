# 韩剧TV 隐私政策

**生效日期：** 2026 年 7 月 13 日

感谢您使用 韩剧TV（以下简称“本应用”）。本政策将向您说明本应用如何收集、使用和保护您的个人信息。请在使用本应用前仔细阅读本政策。

## 一、我们收集的信息

为了向您提供视频浏览、搜索、播放、历史记录、收藏等服务，我们可能会收集以下信息：

1. **设备及网络信息**：包括设备型号、操作系统版本、屏幕分辨率、网络类型（Wi-Fi / 移动网络）、IP 地址等，用于优化视频播放体验和广告展示。
2. **广告标识符（Advertising ID）**：我们使用 Android 广告标识符来向您展示更符合您兴趣的广告，并通过广告平台统计广告效果。
3. **应用使用数据**：包括您浏览或搜索的剧集、播放历史、收藏记录等，主要用于在本地为您提供历史续播、收藏功能。
4. **崩溃日志**：当应用发生异常时，我们可能会在本地保存崩溃堆栈信息，用于排查问题、提升稳定性。崩溃日志仅保存在您的设备本地，除非您主动反馈，否则不会上传。

## 二、我们如何收集信息

1. **本地存储**：您的历史记录、收藏、播放设置（如倍速、自动下一集开关）等数据均存储在您的设备本地数据库（Room）和 SharedPreferences 中，我们不会主动上传到服务器。
2. **广告 SDK**：本应用集成了 Start.io 广告 SDK。Start.io 可能会收集您的设备信息、广告标识符、IP 地址等，用于广告投放和效果分析。具体请参见 [Start.io 隐私政策](https://www.start.io/policy/privacy-policy/)。
3. **数据分析**：本应用目前未集成第三方数据分析 SDK。如有更新，我们将通过本政策告知您。

## 三、广告与第三方服务

本应用使用 Start.io 提供的广告服务，包括横幅广告（Banner）和插屏广告（Interstitial）。这些广告可能会根据您的兴趣和设备信息展示个性化广告。

- 您可以在 Android 系统设置中重置或关闭广告标识符。
- 本应用不会在未成年人专属内容中展示不适宜的广告。

## 四、信息的存储与安全

1. 您的历史记录、收藏等数据仅保存在设备本地，卸载应用后将一并删除。
2. 我们采用行业标准的安全措施保护应用数据，但请您理解，互联网传输和电子存储无法做到 100% 安全。

## 五、儿童隐私

本应用不适合 13 岁以下儿童使用。我们不会故意收集 13 岁以下儿童的个人信息。如果您发现我们收集了儿童个人信息，请联系我们，我们将尽快删除。

## 六、您的权利

您可以随时通过以下方式管理您的数据：

- 在“我的”页面清空历史记录和收藏。
- 在 Android 系统设置中重置广告标识符。
- 卸载应用以删除所有本地数据。

## 七、政策更新

我们可能会不时更新本隐私政策。更新后的政策将在本页面发布，并在应用中提示您查看。如您继续使用本应用，即视为您同意更新后的政策。

## 八、联系我们

如您对本隐私政策有任何疑问，请通过应用内“我的 → 联系开发者”反馈，或发送邮件至：yongzheng784@gmail.com

# HanJu TV Privacy Policy

**Effective Date:** July 13, 2026

Thank you for using HanJu TV (hereinafter referred to as "this App"). This policy explains how this App collects, uses, and protects your personal information. Please read this policy carefully before using the App.

## 1. Information We Collect

To provide you with video browsing, search, playback, history, and favorites services, we may collect the following information:

1. **Device and Network Information**: device model, OS version, screen resolution, network type (Wi-Fi / mobile), IP address, etc., used to optimize video playback and ad delivery.
2. **Advertising ID**: We use the Android Advertising ID to show you more relevant ads and measure ad performance.
3. **App Usage Data**: including the dramas you browse or search, playback history, and favorites, mainly used to provide local resume playback and favorites features.
4. **Crash Logs**: When the App crashes, we may save crash stack traces locally to diagnose issues and improve stability. These logs are stored only on your device unless you voluntarily send them to us.

## 2. How We Collect Information

1. **Local Storage**: Your history, favorites, and playback settings (playback speed, auto-next switch, etc.) are stored in the local Room database and SharedPreferences on your device. We do not actively upload them to our servers.
2. **Advertising SDK**: This App integrates the Start.io advertising SDK. Start.io may collect your device information, advertising ID, IP address, etc., for ad delivery and analytics. Please refer to the [Start.io Privacy Policy](https://www.start.io/policy/privacy-policy/) for details.
3. **Analytics**: This App does not currently integrate third-party analytics SDKs. If this changes, we will notify you through this policy.

## 3. Advertising and Third-Party Services

This App uses Start.io advertising services, including Banner ads and Interstitial ads. These ads may be personalized based on your interests and device information.

- You can reset or disable the Advertising ID in your Android system settings.
- This App will not display inappropriate ads in content intended for minors.

## 4. Data Storage and Security

1. Your history and favorites are stored only on your device and will be deleted when you uninstall the App.
2. We use industry-standard security measures to protect App data. However, please understand that Internet transmission and electronic storage cannot guarantee 100% security.

## 5. Children's Privacy

This App is not intended for children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe we have collected such information, please contact us and we will delete it as soon as possible.

## 6. Your Rights

You can manage your data at any time by:

- Clearing history and favorites in "Profile → Clear History / Clear Favorites".
- Resetting the Advertising ID in Android system settings.
- Uninstalling the App to delete all local data.

## 7. Policy Updates

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. The updated policy will be posted on this page and you will be prompted to review it in the App. Continued use of the App constitutes your acceptance of the updated policy.

## 8. Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please send feedback through "Profile → Contact Developer" in the App, or email us at: yongzheng784@gmail.com
